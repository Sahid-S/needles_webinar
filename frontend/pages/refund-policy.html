Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: November 13, 2025

Important: Please read this policy carefully before making a payment for the Fashion Business Webinar.

1. Registration Fee

The registration fee for the Fashion Business Webinar is ₹1. This is a nominal fee to confirm your participation and secure your seat.

2. Refund Policy

2.1 Participant-Initiated Cancellation

Due to the nominal registration fee and administrative costs, refunds are generally not provided for participant-initiated cancellations. However, exceptions may be considered in the following cases:

  • Medical Emergency: With valid medical documentation
  • Technical Issues: If you are unable to access the webinar due to issues from our end
  • Duplicate Payment: If you accidentally made multiple payments

2.2 Organizer-Initiated Cancellation

If The Needles cancels the webinar due to unforeseen circumstances:

  • Full refund will be processed to all registered participants
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method within 7-10 business days
  • Participants will be notified via email

2.3 Webinar Rescheduling

If the webinar is rescheduled:

  • Your registration remains valid for the new date
  • If you cannot attend the rescheduled date, you may request a refund within 48 hours of the rescheduling notification

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund (for eligible cases), please contact us within 24 hours of registration:

Email: support@theneedles.com
Subject: Refund Request - [Your Name]
Include: Payment ID, Order ID, and reason for refund

4. Refund Processing Time

  • Approval: Refund requests are reviewed within 2-3 business days
  • Processing: Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Credit: The refund amount will be credited to your original payment method
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3-5 business days depending on your bank

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following cases:

  • If you miss the webinar due to your own schedule conflict
  • Technical issues on your end (internet connectivity, device compatibility, etc.)
  • Failure to check email for webinar access details
  • Change of mind after registration
  • If you violate the Terms & Conditions and are removed from the webinar

6. Payment Gateway Charges

In case of refunds, payment gateway charges (if any) are non-refundable and will be deducted from the refund amount.

7. Failed Transactions

If your payment is deducted but the registration is not confirmed:

  • Contact us immediately with your transaction details
  • The amount will be refunded within 7-10 business days if the transaction genuinely failed
  • Most failed transactions are auto-reversed by the bank within 5-7 days
